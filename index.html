Nairobi, Kenya

Kenya is a country located in East Africa, known for its rich cultural diversity, stunning landscapes, and abundant wildlife. It is home to world-famous national parks such as the Maasai Mara, Mount Kenya, and Amboseli, which attract visitors from around the globe. Kenya has a vibrant economy driven by agriculture, tourism, and technology, and Nairobi serves as a major regional hub for business and innovation. The country is also recognized for its long-distance runners, beautiful coastline along the Indian Ocean, and strong traditions rooted in community and heritage.
